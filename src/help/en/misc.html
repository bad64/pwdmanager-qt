Miscellaneous Operations


In addition to the operations described elsewhere in this manual, you can optionally perform the following:

  • Highlight a cell, then press "Copy" to copy the password from that row into your clipboard.
  • Highlight a cell, then press Ctrl+Up to move the entire row up, or Ctrl+Down to move it down.
  • Hide the database in an instant by pressing Ctrl+H. Useful if someone tries to sneak a peek ! Press Ctrl+H to show the database again.
